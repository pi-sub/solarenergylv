Solar Panel

Solar Energy Las Vegas is excited to offer the premier solar pool heating system on the market.

Now available at a reduced rate, TechnoSolis solar panels are visibly thicker, stronger and more durable than other solar pool panels. The reason for this is because the manufacturer uses as much as 35% more raw materials in its panelsthan other manufacturers, which means that they last several years longer, attract more heat and reduce heat loss. In addition, TechnoSolis uses the same high quality polypropylene material throughout the entire panel, which eliminates weak spots caused by expansion and contraction during seasonal temperature changes â€“ an important feature, especially in Las Vegas.

The TechnoSolis solar panel is extra resistant as the solar 104 is molded directly in the piping at both ends of the panel. The interconnected flow tubes prevent heat loss and their oval shape offers a larger heating surface than other systems. Each TechnoSolis solar panel is tested before it leaves the factory, thus ensuring your solar pool heating system is in perfect working order prior to installation.

Another great feature is the manufacturer offers a 10 year warranty, as well as a limited lifetime warranty. Homeowners can rest easy knowing that their pool will be heated for free by the sun, without hassles or headaches, for at least the next decade.

For roof optimization, TechnoSolis solar panels are available in different sizes ranging from from 4-feet x 4.5-feet to 4-feet x 12-feet.

TechnoSolis solar panels have easily surpassed all quality and performance standards set by the various solar testing agencies, such as the Florida Solar Energy Center (FSEC), and the Solar Rating and Certification Corporation (SRCC). The TechnoSolis solar pool panel has the highest BTU* rating on the market with 48,000 BTU* per panel.

*BTU (British Thermal Unit) is a measure of the heat content of fuels or energy sources.
